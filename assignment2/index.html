Artist Bio

Founded in 2020, "Digital Ocean Spaces" is an artistic duo that specialises in creating audiovisual experiences. Consisting of Aaron Alex and Dan Poe, the duo utilises video-rendering software such as Premiere Pro and Touch Designer to create visuals for the music that they make. As of their founding, the pair has made three studio albums, with their latest one selling over 50,000 copies and achieving Platinum status within a month.
