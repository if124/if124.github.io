The Creative Process
Hover over the images for more information!
The Chosen Colours were these three colours. Each of them represents a different feeling. Not listed in any order, they are Nostalgia, Happiness and Love.
The Colour Flowchart involved playing around with these colours in order to see which colours flowed into others better.
The Shapes Experiments were an exercise that had the artist create a whole catalogue of shapes to play around with and let the colour populate it. Doing so enabled the artist to semantically assign each colour to a shape that he desired.
The Test with Magic Music Visuals was done to test just what the software could do. The output from the test was not as desirable as the artist envisioned, so he decided to scrap the idea and utilise another software.
The After Effects Incorporation was a period of enlightenment. This was where the artist managed to find a way to create the work he envisioned. By using After Effects, the chosen colours were able to ebb and flow as intended by him.
The GarageBand Implementation involved getting visuals to work with the visuals created within After Effects. Careful consideration was carried out when deciding on what instruments to use to accompany those visuals. GarageBand was used to allow the artist to see the visuals as they come and play the accompanying audio accordingly.
The GarageBand Plugin called "LABS by Spitfire Audio" was utilised within the tracks created. Auditory attributes such as reverb and sustain was edited using this plugin. The result was a piece of work that allowed resonance to occur within viewers.
